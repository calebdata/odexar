How to Choose a Strong Research Topic

A strong research topic determines the clarity, depth, and overall success of an academic project. Many students begin with broad ideas but struggle to refine them into workable research problems.

The process of selecting a research topic can be approached systematically:

1. Identify a broad area of interest within your field.
2. Review existing literature to understand current discussions.
3. Narrow the focus to a specific issue or gap.
4. Ensure the topic is feasible within your time and resources.
5. Align the topic with academic and practical relevance.

A well-defined topic not only improves the quality of research but also simplifies the processes of data collection, analysis, and interpretation.
